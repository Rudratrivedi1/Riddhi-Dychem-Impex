Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the chemical and pigment industry, Riddhi Dyechem Impex has built a strong legacy of excellence, innovation, and trust. With decades of experience, we are known for delivering top-quality pigments, dyes, and specialty chemicals that meet the dynamic needs of a wide range of industries.

Our journey has been driven by a deep commitment to sustainability, advanced technology, and personalized customer service. From textile to plastics, and coatings to cosmetics, our solutions enhance both the performance and aesthetics of final products.

As the proud parent company of Gomati Chemical Pvt.Ltd., we continue to expand our footprint globally, maintaining high standards of quality, compliance, and reliability. Our team of experts works closely with clients to provide tailor-made solutions that ensure consistency, efficiency, and excellence.
